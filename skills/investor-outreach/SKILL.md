# Investor Outreach Agent Skill

## Purpose
Automates, tracks, and improves communication with potential and current investors for fundraising, deal updates, and follow-up.

## Features
- Manages detailed investor database
- Presents and tracks active deals/opportunities
- Personalized investor email/message templates
- Automated tracking of outreach history and interest levels
- Reminders for follow-up
- Reporting on engagement and fundraising progress

## Config Suggestions
- Investor DB: /data/investor-outreach/investors.csv
- Deal catalog: /data/investor-outreach/opportunities.csv
- Outreach templates: /data/investor-outreach/outreach_templates.md
