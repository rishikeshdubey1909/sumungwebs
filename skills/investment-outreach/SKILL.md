# Investment Outreach Agent Skill

## Purpose
Acts as a client outreach assistant—contacts clients regarding investment opportunities, logs responses, and manages follow-ups.

## Features
- Client contact list management
- Opportunity catalog tracking
- Automated outreach (personalized templates)
- Scheduled follow-up and notes
- Reporting on outreach status/responses

## Config Suggestions
- Client list: /data/investment-outreach/clients.csv
- Opportunities: /data/investment-outreach/opportunities.csv
- Outreach templates: /data/investment-outreach/outreach_templates.md

## Usage
- Add/update client info and interests
- List new investment opportunities
- Use templates for batch or personalized outreach
- Log follow-ups, responses, and next steps
